Schedules

Overview

Schedules run preview-generation jobs on a timer so you don't have to kick them off by hand. Pick the scan mode that matches what you want:

  • Full library scan — walk an entire Plex library and generate previews for anything missing. Good for weekly/nightly runs.
  • Recently added only — fast poll that just checks what Plex added in the last few minutes. Good for tight, frequent intervals (every 15 min) when you want fresh imports to get previews quickly without scanning everything. See the Webhooks page for a one-click "Create default scanner" shortcut.
You can have as many schedules as you like — e.g. "Nightly full scan of Movies at 2 AM" and "Recently Added scan every 15 minutes with 1h lookback" can coexist.
