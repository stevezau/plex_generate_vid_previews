Overview

Schedules run preview-generation jobs on a timer so you don't have to kick them off by hand. Pick the scan mode that matches what you want:

  • Full library scan — walk an entire library on the selected server (Plex, Emby, or Jellyfin) and generate previews for anything missing. Good for weekly/nightly runs.
  • Recently added only — fast poll that just checks what was added in the last few minutes (works for Plex, Emby, and Jellyfin). Good for tight, frequent intervals (every 15 min) when you want fresh imports to get previews quickly without scanning everything. If your downloader (Sonarr/Radarr/Tdarr) can fire a webhook on import, the Triggers tab is even faster — it fires the moment new media lands.
You can have as many schedules as you like — e.g. "Nightly full scan of Movies at 2 AM" and "Recently Added scan every 15 minutes with 1h lookback" can coexist.
